Why Now

Market conditions and regulatory requirements are creating new demands for edge-based inspection systems.

Increasing Inspection Frequency

Infrastructure operators are expanding monitoring coverage to reduce downtime and extend asset life. Traditional manual inspection cycles are being supplemented with continuous automated surveillance.

Rising Regulatory Documentation

Compliance frameworks now require detailed records of inspection findings, timestamps, and sensor metadata. Documentation burdens are increasing across energy, transportation, and utilities sectors.

Escalating Data Volumes

High-resolution video capture generates terabytes of inspection data annually. Storage and transmission costs are becoming significant operational expenses, particularly in remote environments.

Maturing Edge Compute Hardware

Low-power processors with dedicated acceleration are now available at price points suitable for embedded deployment. Real-time processing at the edge is technically and economically feasible.

About AuraSense

AuraSense develops real-time inspection middleware for infrastructure operators. The platform integrates with existing drone hardware and analytics stacks to provide edge-based anomaly detection and control.

The system processes video streams on embedded processors, reducing bandwidth requirements and enabling immediate response to detected anomalies. Compressed event representations are transmitted to cloud storage and analytics systems for further processing.

Deployments are designed to fit within existing operational workflows, requiring minimal changes to flight planning, sensor configurations, or downstream analytics infrastructure.
